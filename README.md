# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Churn Analysis in European Bank

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **customer churn in a European bank**, with the goal of identifying key factors that drive customer attrition and financial loss.

It demonstrates a complete **end-to-end data analytics pipeline**, including:

* Data cleaning using Excel
* Data analysis using SQL
* Data visualization using Power BI

The analysis goes beyond basic metrics by incorporating **customer segmentation, risk analysis, and revenue impact evaluation**, making it highly relevant for real-world business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify customer segments with high churn risk
* Analyze behavioral and financial patterns of churned customers
* Measure **revenue loss due to churn**
* Provide actionable insights to improve customer retention

## 📈 Key Insights

* High-balance customers contribute the **maximum revenue loss**
* Inactive customers are the **primary drivers of churn**
* Customers with 3–4 products have higher churn risk
* Germany shows both **high churn and high financial impact**
* Mid-age customers (30–60) are more likely to churn
* Customer engagement is a key retention factor

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Prioritize retention of **high-value inactive customers**
* Improve engagement strategies (personalized offers, alerts)
* Target mid-term customers with retention campaigns
* Offer optimized product bundles
* Implement region-specific strategies for high-risk areas
